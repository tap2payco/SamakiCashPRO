## **1\. Executive Summary**

**Project Name:** Samaki Pro

**Mission:** To industrialize the Lake Victoria fishing industry by merging real-time biological data with advanced financial tools. Samaki Pro aims to bridge the $1.3 billion to $1.7 billion annual agricultural credit gap in Tanzania 1 by providing a JavaScript-based "operating system" for cage farmers, vendors, and cold-chain providers in the Mwanza region.

## ---

**2\. Integrated Feature Ecosystem**

### **A. Core Fintech Modules (Transaction & Credit)**

* **"Genetic" Agent Suite (JavaScript/Node.js):**  
  * **Market Insight Agent:** Scrapes real-time price trends from Mwanza’s Kirumba and Mwaloni markets to prevent price manipulation.2  
  * **Matchmaking & Negotiation Agent:** Uses the geneticalgorithm JS library and Gemini API to pair farmers with high-value vendors based on harvest cycles and supply-demand shocks.  
* **Escrow & Smart Payments:** A Node.js-based service that holds vendor funds until a digital "Proof of Quality" is signed upon delivery.  
* **Digital Credit Scoring:** Uses IoT data (fish growth rates/water quality) to create an "Economic Identity" for farmers, enabling loans for fingerlings and feed without traditional collateral.

### **B. Core AIoT Modules (The "Brain")**

* **AI Vision (TensorFlow.js):** Cameras monitor fish behavior to detect "satiety" and optimize feeding, directly addressing the 60–70% feed cost burden.3  
* **Real-time Sensor Monitoring:** Cloud-integrated sensors for **Water pH, Temperature, and Dissolved Oxygen (DO)**. The system triggers instant mobile alerts via MQTT to prevent stock loss.

### **C. Advanced Value-Added Modules**

* **Parametric "Climate-Bio" Insurance:** Triggers automatic payouts to farmers if sensors detect lethal DO/pH levels or if meteorological data confirms extreme weather.  
* **Solar Cold Chain Asset Financing:** A "Pay-As-You-Go" (PAYG) model for solar-powered storage units managed by smart contracts.  
* **Blockchain "Export Passports":** A traceability ledger providing QR codes for every batch, proving sustainability for international markets as the SGR Phase 5 connects Mwanza to global routes.  
* **Blue Carbon Credit Marketplace:** Tokenizes sustainable farming practices into carbon credits for secondary "green" revenue.

## ---

**3\. Development Roadmap (Target Launch: July 2026\)**

### **Phase 1: Foundation & Regulatory Compliance (Jan – Feb 2026\)**

* **Legal:** Register as a **Data Controller** with the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) by April 2025 to comply with the PDPA 2023\.5  
* **Licensing:** Apply for the **Bank of Tanzania (BoT) Fintech Regulatory Sandbox** (Cohort 2\) to test escrow and P2P lending models.  
* **Branding:** Complete the transition from Samaki Cash to Samaki Pro.  
* **Architecture:** Set up the Node.js/NestJS backend and PostgreSQL database.

### **Phase 2: MVP Development – Marketplace & Payments (Mar – Apr 2026\)**

* **Marketplace:** Build the React Native mobile app for vendors and farmers to list and discover stock.  
* **Financial Engine:** Develop the Escrow service in Node.js and integrate with local payment boards (Mobile Money/TIPS).  
* **Genetic Agent Alpha:** Implement the first iteration of the pricing agent using historical data from Mwaloni market.2

### **Phase 3: AIoT Prototype & Data Integration (May – June 2026\)**

* **Sensor Alpha:** Deploy ESP32-based sensors (pH/Temp/DO) to a pilot group of 5 cage farmers in Buchosa/Ilemela.  
* **AI Vision Beta:** Train TensorFlow.js models on fish feeding patterns to reduce feed waste.  
* **Credit Scoring Beta:** Begin generating "Digital Footprints" for pilot farmers using real-time biomass data.  
* **Insurance Registration:** Register as an **Insurance Digital Platform (IDP)** with TIRA, securing the mandatory TZS 100M Professional Indemnity cover.

### **Phase 4: Full Scale Launch & Expansion (July 2026 – Beyond)**

* **Commercial Launch:** Release Samaki Pro at the conclusion of the C. Hub training program.  
* **Logistics Integration:** Align marketplace schedules with the **MV Mwanza** ferry and the **SGR Phase 5** freight services to optimize "Lake-to-Sea" exports.6  
* **Sustainability Modules:** Launch the Blue Carbon and Blockchain Traceability modules once the core marketplace is stable.

**4\. Technical Stack**

| Layer | Technology |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Mobile App** | **React Native** (Cross-platform) |
| **Backend** | **Node.js (NestJS)** for real-time concurrency |
| **IoT Connectivity** | **MQTT** protocol \+ ESP32 Hardware |
| **AI / Logic** | **Gemini 1.5 Pro API** (Agents) & **TensorFlow.js** (Vision) |
| **Database** | **PostgreSQL** (Transactions) & **InfluxDB** (IoT data) |
| **Blockchain** | **Solana** or **Hyperledger** (Traceability) |

**5\. Conclusion**

By integrating core fintech transactions with advanced AIoT monitoring, Samaki Pro will become the financial backbone of the Lake Victoria Blue Economy. This roadmap ensures that by July 2026, the platform will be legally compliant, technically robust, and strategically positioned to leverage Mwanza's massive infrastructure upgrades.8
